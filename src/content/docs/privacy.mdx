---
title: Privacy
description: How the DottoUI documentation site handles your data.
---

The DottoUI documentation site is a static website. It does not collect personal
data, set advertising cookies, or run third-party analytics.

## What is stored

- **Theme preference** - your light/dark choice is saved in your browser's
  `localStorage` (`starlight-theme`) so the site remembers it across visits. It
  never leaves your device.

## Third-party requests

- **GitHub Pages** - the site is hosted on GitHub Pages and subject to
  [GitHub's privacy statement](https://docs.github.com/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement).
